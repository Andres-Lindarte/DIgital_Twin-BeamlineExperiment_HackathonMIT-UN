SIMION Non-Ideal Grid Examples

This folder contains two sub-examples demonstrating different ways of handling non-ideals grids in SIMION. If the grid contains a large number of holes that is too complex to model as a single PA, the general approach is to model a small section of that grid and then effectively repeat it. However, there are a few different ways to "repeat it."

  • nonideal_grid\pa_jump - jumps particles into the PA representing the small section of the grid.
  • nonideal_grid\particle_jump - jumps the PA representing the small section of the grid to wherever particles are located. (This requires SIMION 8.1 or above.)
