SIMION 8.1 Examples (Documentation)

These are descriptions of the examples distributed with SIMION.

  • bender_cut - split and crop a system into two arrays.
  • buncher - Buncher lens
  • bradbury_nielsen_grid - Bradbury-Nielson Grid (Gate/Shutter) system split into coarse and fine arrays [8.1]
  • child_particles - Replaces parent particles with multiple child particles.
  • collision_hs1 - Complex hard-sphere ion-gas collision model (HS1).
  • collision_sds - Statistically Simulated Diffusion (SDS) Collision Model - handles atmospheric pressure.
  • collision_rs - collision_sds adapted for chemical reaction simulation (RS)
  • contour - plotting contour and field plots with a user program
  • count_transmission - calculation % transmission (percent particles hitting electrode) with user program
  • dielectric - solving electric fields with dielectrics, as well as solving current density with conductivity and electric fields with floating conductors
  • drag - Stokes' law viscosity
  • einzel - Einzel lens
  • electrostatic_induction - compute (and plot) the charge and current induced on an electrode by particles flying in space. [8.1]
  • emittance - Emittance calcalation for a beam
  • excel - Plotting in Excel
  • extension - Extension modules. Calling C or C++ from Lua.
  • faims - Field Asymmetric Waveform Ion Mobility Spectrometry (FAIMS) examples, with SDS-FAIMS collision model
  • field - Generates a magnetic field PA from a set of field vectors (SL Libraries)
  • field_array - Magnetic field grid loaded and interpolated from ASCII text file
  • field_emission - field emission cathode field calculation and ray tracing.
  • gauss_law - calculating total charge (or capacitance) inside volume via Gauss's Law[1] or via total field energy (SL Libraries or PA API) [8.0/8.1]
  • geometry - Various geometry definition examples (GEM and STL)
  • geometry_optimization - Geometry optimization
  • gnuplot - Plotting data in the gnuplot program (simlar to Excel example) [8.1]
  • hda - Hemispherical Deflection Analyzer (HDA) - with simplex voltage optimization and plotting
  • helmholtz coil - helmholtz coil magnetic field field calculation via Biot-Savart
  • icrcell - Ion Cyclotron Resonance (ICR)
  • ims - Ion Mobility Spectrometry (IMS) example
  • ionfunnel - RF ion funnel.
  • kingdon_trap - Kingdon trap / quadro-logarithmic field / orbitrap (electrostatic trap) example.
  • lens_properties - electrostatic lens properties, calculating and plotting
  • magnet - Magnet examples
  • magnetic_potential - Poisson solving magnetic scalar and vector potentials with current density and/or permeability distributions in space.
  • magnetic_sector - Magnetic sector examples
  • matlab - Exporting from MATLAB®/Octave to SIMION PA's and other interfacing examples. [8.2]
  • mfield_adjust - programmatically-defined magnetic field (mfield_adjust segment) demonstration
  • misc - Miscellaneous examples
  • molecular_beam - beam of neutral molecules with dipoles [8.2]
  • multipole_expansion - of unknown potentials [8.1]
  • multiprocess - teleporting particles between multiple communicating SIMION processes
  • nonideal_grid/particle_jump - non-ideal grid, particle and PA jumping tricks
  • octupole - RF Octupole ion guide
  • pa2text - Simple example of reading PA with SL Libraries
  • pseudopotential calculation/plot for RF trap
  • performance_tests - performance tests
  • plot - Plotting in Excel and gnuplot
  • poisson - extension of SIMION's Refine function to handle space-charge density distributions (Poisson equation solver) [8.1] Includes subexamples: static_charge pipe_with_charge (static charge distributions), pipe_with_beam, pierce_gun (particle-in-cell, PIC, self-consistent trajectories and fields), octupole_sc (RF octupole space-charge),
  • quad - RF Quadrupole
  • random - Particle beam randomization
  • random_fly2 - passes adjustable variables to a FLY2 file.
  • repulsion - space-charge repulsion examples [8.0/8.1]
  • resistive - continuous voltage gradients (resistive material) on PA electrodes.
  • rfdemo - RF demos
  • secondary - Secondary emissions
  • sims - Secondary ion mass spectromerty (SIMS)
  • solenoid - Air core solenoid magnetic field calculation via Biot-Savart
  • spectrum - Demonstrates voltage scan and acquisition and plotting of spectrum
  • surface_enhancement - PA surface enhancement features in Refine for order-of-magnitude improvement in field accuracy [8.1]
  • swirl - Example of writing a PA with the SL Libraries
  • test_plane - Applies user program to record data on multiple non-orthogonal test planes
  • test_sphere - Applies user program to record data on spherical test surface (similar to test_plane example but with sphere)
  • tof - Time of flight (TOF) mass spec
  • trap - RF ion trap
  • tune - Voltage optimization (lens tuning)
  • tandem_van_def_graaff - Tandem Van de Graaff, with stripping charge through a thin foil [SECONDARY]
  • waveform - applies abitrary segmented waveforms to any number of electrodes (uses a waveformlib.lua library to handle much of the complexity) [8.1]

By Topic

  • simple electrostatics: einzel | misc | tof (multiple PA) | sims | kingdon_trap
  • magnetic fields - simple magnetostatics: magnet | magnetic sector
  • magnetic fields - Biot-Savart (wire current): helmholtz coil | solenoid
  • magnetic fields - scalar potential, vector potential, current density, and/or permeability magnetic_potential
  • magnetic fields - other: mfield_adjust | field_array |
  • dielectrics: dielectric
  • floating conductors: dielectric | gauss_law
  • current density/resistive: dielectric (from conductivities) | resistive (electrode voltage gradient) | magnetic_potential (bfields from)
  • RF voltages: rfdemo | octupole | quad | trap | icrcell | ionfunnel | waveform | buncher (pulse) | pseudopotential | poisson\octupole_sc (RF octupole space-charge) | surface_enhancement (high accuracy)
  • gas collision models: drag | collision_hs1 | collision_sds | collision_rs | faims (SDS-FAIMS) | ionfunnel | ims | trap
  • child/secondary/redefined particles/surfaces: child_particles | secondary | tandem_van_def_graaff | random | random_fly2 | field_emission
  • multiple PAs: tof | quad | bender_cut (crop) | bradbury_nielsen_grid | field_emission (nested)
  • field accuracy, curved or fine surfaces: surface_enhancement | field_emission (nested)
  • grids: nonideal_grid | bradbury_nielsen_grid
  • plotting/contouring: contour | plot | excel | gnuplot | spectrum
  • data recording/analysis: count_transmission | test_plane | test_sphere | pa2text | multipole_expansion | gauss_law
  • beam properties: emittance | lens_properties
  • remote program communication: extension | multiprocess
  • ICR (penning trap): icrcell | electrostatic_induction
  • paul trap: see RF voltages
  • electrostatic trap: kingdon_trap
  • IMS: ims | faims | ionfunnel
  • optimization: tune | geometry_optimization | lens_properties | spectrum | hda
  • geometry definition: geometry | swirl | kingdon_trap (analytical)
  • space charge: repulsion | poisson
  • interfacing to other programs: excel | gnuplot | plot | matlab | misc | extension | field_array
  • special forces on particles: drag | repulsion | poisson | molecular_beam | poisson/pipe_with_beam (bfieldearth/gravity)

There are additional examples in the ASMS courses included with SIMION. See also the SIMION Info site online.
